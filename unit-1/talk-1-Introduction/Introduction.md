# CodeGirl Computing Camp

CodeGirl is a Computing Summer Camp designed to encourage female secondary school students to explore careers in Information and Communication Technology (ICT).

The camp will introduce participants to a range of computing areas in a fun, interactive way. Students will gain hands‑on experience in practical Python coding and web development, explore career paths in tech, and take part in industry visits.
